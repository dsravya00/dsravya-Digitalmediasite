Exercise 5 - Phase 1 & 2

In Exercise 4, For Project #3, Phase 2, I created four custom functions and developed a map of two arrays. I designed four unique images of LEGO pieces, each featuring a face that expresses a different emotion: one with teeth, another with sunglasses, one with hair, and one with a smile. I also included several commonly used words. Additionally, our group decided to organize these images into a 16x16 array grid for a structured and cohesive layout. My team members are Alex, Jared, and Sravya, and we chose the theme of “Heads/Faces/Emotes.” I created three slides and arranged them sequentially, incorporating the variables of my code, jared code and alexcode in slides one through three.

Project 3

In Project 3, displaying time in different way was challenging, and it took me some time to understand how seconds, minutes, and hours interacted, especially when connecting the draw function and labels in the code. For the clock visualization, I chose to illustrate water dropping and filling a container. One aspect that went well was organizing and arranging the elements to produce the output. The main difficulty, however, was incorporating and previewing daily activities for each hour, which I used in my presentation.
